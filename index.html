History Map

Remember when the Go-Go was EVERYWHERE? Outdoor parties, the old DC clubs where we couldn't even drive but somehow always got in... That sound, from Georgia Ave to Anacostia to Maryland and VA, pocket came alive until the AM. Chuck Brown, Experience Unlimited, Trouble Funk and Rare Essence blessing stages in places we still walk past today.
